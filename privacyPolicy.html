Privacy Policy

Welcome to use Multiform (hereinafter referred to as "this app" or "service")

We are well aware of the importance of privacy to you, so in the design and development process of this app, we have always been committed to protecting your personal privacy. This privacy policy aims to explain to you how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information, particularly in our practices regarding album and camera permissions, as well as iCloud service integration. Please carefully read the following privacy policy before using this app and ensure that you fully understand its content.

1、 Privacy protection principle

No user personal information collection:

This app does not collect, store or share any personal information about you, including but not limited to name, contact information, location information, etc. We have not set up a registration and login function, nor have we established a separate database to store user information.

Minimize permission request:

We only apply for necessary permissions to support the core functions of this app, namely album and camera permissions, which are granted entirely based on your voluntary choice. We will not abuse these permissions, nor will we use your personal information for any purposes unrelated to the functionality of the app.

2、 Application and use of album and camera permissions

When you choose to select pictures from your album or use your camera to take new pictures as mind map material, we will request access to your album or camera. These permissions are granted to enable you to easily add image materials to mind maps. We promise that these image materials will only be used for your creations on this app and stored on your device or in your iCloud account (if applicable).

3、 Integration and Data Storage of iCloud Services

The use of iCloud services:

In order to enhance the user experience, this app integrates iCloud services, allowing you to securely store your created mind maps and the image materials they contain on iCloud. This way, you can seamlessly synchronize and access your mind map across multiple devices.

Data Privacy and Security:

When you choose to use iCloud to store data, your mind maps and image materials will be encrypted and stored on iCloud servers. Only you have access to this data, and we will not access, analyze, or share it in any form.

5、 Update of Privacy Policy

We may revise this privacy policy based on changes in laws and regulations or updates to the functionality of this app.
